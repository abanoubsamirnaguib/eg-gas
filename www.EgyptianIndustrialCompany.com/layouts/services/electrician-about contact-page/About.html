About Us

Here at egyptian industrial company, we are a full-service global manufacturing and supply chain . We offer non ferrous metal pressure die casting mainly aluminum alloy and zinc alloy .

Its our mission to exceed your expectations we always keep your production goals in mind.

Our Story

Providing Industrial Services Since 2005

Egyptian Industrial Company was incorporated in 2005. The company is located in New Cairo industrial zone.The company has evolved itself as a manufacturer and supplier of high pressure aluminium die casting and machined components.

The company is managed by a team of professionally trained managers and engineers and the entire team focuses towards customer satisfaction and upgrades itself to match the changing customer requirements. The team takes every care to ensure the quality of components at each stage of process and ensure defect free product is passed on to the customers.
